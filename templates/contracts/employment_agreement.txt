EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into on this [DATE] day of [MONTH], [YEAR]

BETWEEN:

[COMPANY NAME], a company incorporated under the laws of India, having its registered office at [ADDRESS] (hereinafter referred to as the "Company" or "Employer", which expression shall, unless repugnant to the context or meaning thereof, include its successors and assigns) of the FIRST PART;

AND

[EMPLOYEE NAME], aged [AGE] years, residing at [ADDRESS], holding PAN [PAN NUMBER] (hereinafter referred to as the "Employee", which expression shall, unless repugnant to the context or meaning thereof, include his/her heirs, executors, and administrators) of the SECOND PART.

The Company and the Employee are hereinafter individually referred to as "Party" and collectively as "Parties".

WHEREAS:

A. The Company is engaged in the business of [BUSINESS DESCRIPTION];
B. The Employee possesses the qualifications, skills, and experience required by the Company;
C. The Company desires to employ the Employee and the Employee desires to be employed by the Company on the terms and conditions set forth herein.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements herein contained, the Parties agree as follows:

1. DEFINITIONS AND INTERPRETATION

1.1 In this Agreement, unless the context otherwise requires:
   (a) "Confidential Information" means all information relating to the business, operations, technology, customers, and affairs of the Company;
   (b) "Effective Date" means [DATE];
   (c) "Intellectual Property" means all patents, copyrights, trademarks, trade secrets, and other intellectual property rights;
   (d) "Salary" means the gross monthly compensation payable to the Employee.

2. POSITION AND DUTIES

2.1 The Company hereby employs the Employee in the position of [DESIGNATION] with effect from the Effective Date.

2.2 The Employee shall report to [REPORTING MANAGER/DESIGNATION].

2.3 The Employee shall perform such duties as may be assigned by the Company from time to time, including but not limited to:
   (a) [DUTY 1];
   (b) [DUTY 2];
   (c) [DUTY 3];
   (d) Such other duties as may be reasonably assigned.

2.4 The Employee shall devote full time, attention, and abilities to the business of the Company during working hours.

3. COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS

3.1 The Company shall pay the Employee a gross annual salary of INR [AMOUNT] (Rupees [AMOUNT IN WORDS] only), payable in twelve (12) equal monthly installments.

3.2 The salary shall be subject to applicable tax deductions at source as per the Income Tax Act, 1961.

3.3 The Employee shall be entitled to the following benefits:
   (a) Provident Fund contribution as per applicable laws;
   (b) Gratuity as per the Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972;
   (c) Medical insurance coverage of INR [AMOUNT];
   (d) [OTHER BENEFITS].

3.4 Annual salary reviews shall be conducted at the discretion of the Company based on performance.

4. WORKING HOURS AND LOCATION

4.1 The normal working hours shall be [X] hours per day, [Y] days per week, excluding lunch breaks.

4.2 The Employee may be required to work additional hours as necessary for the proper performance of duties without additional compensation.

4.3 The primary place of work shall be [LOCATION]. The Company may require the Employee to work at other locations as needed.

5. LEAVE AND HOLIDAYS

5.1 The Employee shall be entitled to:
   (a) [X] days of Earned Leave per annum;
   (b) [Y] days of Sick Leave per annum;
   (c) [Z] days of Casual Leave per annum;
   (d) Public holidays as declared by the Company.

5.2 Leave shall be availed as per the Company's leave policy in effect from time to time.

5.3 Unused Earned Leave may be carried forward or encashed as per Company policy.

6. PROBATION PERIOD

6.1 The Employee shall be on probation for a period of [X] months from the Effective Date.

6.2 During the probation period, either Party may terminate this Agreement by giving [Y] days' written notice.

6.3 Upon successful completion of the probation period, the Employee shall be confirmed in writing.

7. CONFIDENTIALITY

7.1 The Employee acknowledges that during the course of employment, the Employee will have access to Confidential Information.

7.2 The Employee agrees to:
   (a) Maintain strict confidentiality of all Confidential Information;
   (b) Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent;
   (c) Use Confidential Information solely for the purposes of employment;
   (d) Return all Confidential Information upon termination.

7.3 The obligations under this clause shall survive the termination of this Agreement for a period of [X] years.

8. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

8.1 All Intellectual Property created by the Employee during the course of employment shall be the exclusive property of the Company.

8.2 The Employee hereby assigns to the Company all rights, title, and interest in any Intellectual Property created during employment.

8.3 The Employee shall execute all documents necessary to perfect the Company's ownership of such Intellectual Property.

9. NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION

9.1 During the term of employment and for a period of [X] months thereafter, the Employee shall not:
   (a) Engage in any business that directly competes with the Company within [GEOGRAPHIC AREA];
   (b) Solicit or attempt to solicit any customer or client of the Company;
   (c) Solicit or attempt to solicit any employee of the Company.

9.2 The restrictions in this clause are reasonable and necessary for the protection of the Company's legitimate business interests.

10. TERMINATION

10.1 After the probation period, either Party may terminate this Agreement by giving [X] days' written notice or salary in lieu thereof.

10.2 The Company may terminate this Agreement immediately without notice for cause, including but not limited to:
   (a) Gross misconduct or insubordination;
   (b) Breach of any material term of this Agreement;
   (c) Conviction of a criminal offense;
   (d) Fraud, dishonesty, or misappropriation;
   (e) Violation of Company policies.

10.3 Upon termination, the Employee shall:
   (a) Return all Company property, documents, and Confidential Information;
   (b) Complete all pending work and handover responsibilities;
   (c) Settle all outstanding dues and advances.

11. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

11.1 Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall first be attempted to be resolved through mutual negotiation.

11.2 If the dispute is not resolved within [X] days, it shall be referred to arbitration in accordance with the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.

11.3 The arbitration shall be conducted by a sole arbitrator mutually appointed by the Parties.

11.4 The seat of arbitration shall be [CITY], India.

11.5 The language of arbitration shall be English.

12. GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION

12.1 This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India.

12.2 Subject to the arbitration clause, the courts of [CITY] shall have exclusive jurisdiction.

13. GENERAL PROVISIONS

13.1 Entire Agreement: This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties and supersedes all prior negotiations and agreements.

13.2 Amendment: This Agreement may only be amended by a written instrument signed by both Parties.

13.3 Severability: If any provision is held invalid, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

13.4 Waiver: No waiver of any breach shall constitute a waiver of any subsequent breach.

13.5 Notices: All notices shall be in writing and delivered to the addresses specified herein.
