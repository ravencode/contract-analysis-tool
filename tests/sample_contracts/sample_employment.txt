EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into on this 15th day of January, 2026

BETWEEN:

TechVision Solutions Private Limited, a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, having its registered office at 42, Electronic City Phase 1, Bangalore, Karnataka - 560100 (hereinafter referred to as the "Company" or "Employer") of the FIRST PART;

AND

Rahul Sharma, aged 28 years, residing at 156, Koramangala 4th Block, Bangalore, Karnataka - 560034, holding PAN ABCPS1234K (hereinafter referred to as the "Employee") of the SECOND PART.

WHEREAS:

A. The Company is engaged in the business of software development and IT consulting services;
B. The Employee possesses the qualifications and experience required by the Company;
C. The Company desires to employ the Employee as a Senior Software Engineer.

NOW, THEREFORE, the Parties agree as follows:

1. POSITION AND DUTIES

1.1 The Company hereby employs the Employee in the position of Senior Software Engineer with effect from February 1, 2026.

1.2 The Employee shall report to the Engineering Manager.

1.3 The Employee shall perform software development, code review, and technical mentoring duties as assigned.

2. COMPENSATION

2.1 The Company shall pay the Employee a gross annual salary of INR 18,00,000 (Rupees Eighteen Lakhs only), payable in monthly installments.

2.2 The Employee shall be entitled to:
   (a) Provident Fund contribution as per EPF Act;
   (b) Medical insurance coverage of INR 5,00,000;
   (c) Annual performance bonus up to 15% of annual salary.

3. WORKING HOURS

3.1 The normal working hours shall be 9 hours per day, 5 days per week.

3.2 The Employee may be required to work additional hours without additional compensation.

4. LEAVE

4.1 The Employee shall be entitled to 24 days of Earned Leave, 12 days of Sick Leave, and 6 days of Casual Leave per annum.

5. PROBATION

5.1 The Employee shall be on probation for 6 months from the date of joining.

5.2 During probation, either Party may terminate with 15 days' notice.

6. CONFIDENTIALITY

6.1 The Employee shall maintain strict confidentiality of all proprietary information, trade secrets, client data, and business information of the Company.

6.2 This obligation shall survive termination for a period of 3 years.

7. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

7.1 All intellectual property created by the Employee during employment, whether during or outside working hours, shall be the exclusive property of the Company.

7.2 The Employee hereby irrevocably assigns all rights to such intellectual property to the Company.

8. NON-COMPETE

8.1 During employment and for 24 months thereafter, the Employee shall not engage in any business that competes with the Company anywhere in India.

8.2 The Employee shall not solicit any customer or employee of the Company for 24 months after termination.

9. TERMINATION

9.1 After probation, either Party may terminate with 90 days' written notice or salary in lieu.

9.2 The Company may terminate immediately without notice for:
   (a) Gross misconduct;
   (b) Breach of confidentiality;
   (c) Poor performance;
   (d) Any reason at the Company's sole discretion.

9.3 Upon termination, the Employee shall return all Company property and shall not be entitled to any severance pay.

10. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

10.1 Any dispute shall be referred to arbitration in Singapore under ICC Rules.

10.2 The decision of the arbitrator shall be final and binding with no right of appeal.

11. GOVERNING LAW

11.1 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.

11.2 The courts of Bangalore shall have jurisdiction.

12. GENERAL

12.1 The Company may amend this Agreement at its sole discretion with 30 days' notice.

12.2 The Employee waives all claims against the Company arising from employment.
